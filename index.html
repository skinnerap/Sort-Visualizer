Sorting Algorithm Visualizer

How to Use:

Visualize popular sorting algorithms by simply clicking the button at the top of the page that corresponds to the sorting algorithm that you wish to see.

Adjust the amount of lines to sort, and the speed of sorting by adjusting the appropriate sliders on the top of the page.

Click the Randomize Data button to create a new randomized list of lines to sort!
